AERO UNWIRED

Aero Unwired is a group of enthusiasts from the Unwired family eveready to take that extra mile. Ever since its inception, the team has grown in leaps and bounds with a variety of innovative projects in its quiver. The team has successfully developed Photography Drones, Semi Autonomous Surveillance Drones, Powered Gliders, FPV Airplanes, IC Engine Airplanes and Quadcopters.

With a vision to achieve greater heights and put to test the knowledge gained over the yaers, the team is now all set with a number of upcoming projects on the chart. Tricoptor, Ornithopter and Autogyro are some on the list.
